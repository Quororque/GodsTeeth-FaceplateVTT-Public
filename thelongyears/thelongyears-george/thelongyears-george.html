How did you cope?

You cope with loss, grief, regret. You don't cope with whatever the fuck happened there. The worst part of that whole thing was is that I went under the impression of it having been my own choice and came out realizing that nothing was. I tried to reclaim control by obsessively volunteering to help various charity programs, whatever was in the community. Helping people on my own terms, but barely making the rest of my life work. Just zipping from one place to another trying to tie up ends.

What's the new normal?

The new normal for me was being so busy I didn't have a moment's time to think. Endless renovations around the house, activities and random hobbies acquired and discarded the moment they started becoming routine.
