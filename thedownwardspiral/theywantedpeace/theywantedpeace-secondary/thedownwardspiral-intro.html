The Downward Spiral: 2016-2026

It is September 19, 2026. Everything has changed. It doesn't matter whether the original Teeth have lost their lives. They were disposable to the force that controls all of them, thus they have been replaced, and the cycle marches onward. Knowing about fatalism cannot disarm it. When the collection is due, the clock hits September 2026. Many of the special vignettes take between December 2019 and January 2021. The global pandemic was just a mental exercise, until everyone on Earth had to live through it. Some people still hope that Americans will agree to contain the outbreak. Millions are dead due to misinformation and lack of initiative, millions more left injured to vaccines. The full, lethal extent of the corruption within the United States is only beginning to become plain. The Teeth, seemingly better at detecting and neutralizing unnatural threats, are used extensively by the Program. Primarily they are used to confirm the existence of the threats, and at worst case scenarios, put them down. Threats that already consumed good agents. The Program rearranged the Teeth to new postings and declared Working Group MASTICATE to be a permanent assignment in the form of S-cell. To prevent losing all operatives to a single threat, they are often sent in singles and pairs alongside caretaker teams tasked with maintaining the "usefulness" of the "assets". Though nebulously debriefed on what the Teeth are capable of, they are given explicit instructions to watch for any unnatural threat in the "asset" itself and shoot them in the face if they see it. Despite their advancing age, the Teeth find themselves in regular debriefing interviews where they go over the development of their powers with Program staff and doctors. God's New Teeth

The Downward Spiral is in a similar vein to the previous The Long Years section. Most of the rules are similar, but with some new faces.
  • (Original Teeth) How did you cope?
How is the empty husk of a life you still have holding up? Did you get a divorce yet? Maybe a second, or third divorce? Do you still live in a house, or are you just crashing in motels or sleeping in the back of your car? Do your children talk to you? None of this matters to anyone but the little human you have left in yourself.
  • (New Teeth) How were you recruited?
You weren't expecting an internal reassignment within the Program given how compartmentalized it is. Most Agents live and die knowing only one or two contacts. You were made to join this well-established entourage, but under what circumstances?
  • Where were you during COVID-19?
The pandemic changed everything. Your character will forever remember where they were when they heard that the lockdown is being extended indefinitely. They can narrate how their jobs changed, or how the pandemic led to promotion and advancement. But does it even matter in the end, compared to the jaws of life we are all stuck inside?
  • What kept you going?
For the Original Teeth, it's extraordinary that someone hasn't took their own life yet after learning the terrible truth of the reality they exist in. You're still sane as in you're not flailing in a padded cell screaming about the hungry universe to a disinterested psychiatrist. But really, could you be considered sane or something more of in control for now? Describe your personal stakes in this, the reason why you haven't just decided to tap out from this disgusting existence. For the New Teeth the pandemic was a time of great hardship, socially and economically. Describe these blunder years and how you somehow managed to not drop out of the rat race in some way, avoiding new mental illnesses and addictions that consumed so many people. (edited)
09:30
  • How did you make yourself useful?
Much of the work your character did for the Program is alone or in pairs. Every week or two, they are called in for a debriefing interview with the Program staff. Your character investigates points of interest to confirm whether that awful gingerbread smell is emanating from it. You occasionally figure out that it's just bogus or humans being humans in the worst ways, and those sorts are handed off to someone else while you are sent home. How does your character approach this work? How do they respond to it? Describe your new life as a hunting dog.
  • (Old Teeth) Choose three (3x at once) home pursuits!
You must pick three individual (may repeat) home pursuits from Page 78 of Agent's Handbook and detail them. You may also imagine your own pursuit outside of these ones, it will likely make sense. Typically, “home” scenes occur between missions and reflect months or even years of time. They are a chance to explore the most important things in an Agent’s life, and to confirm that those things are spiraling down. Remember: combat skills are limited by your occupation and cannot be trained further unless you have a significant change of career. Crafting a characters entire personal life in these short scenes differs from the more procedural elements of play. It might call for large time skips and parallel narratives. Keep this tip in mind. Limitations

Below are non-negotiable limitations to your (back)story that you are required to follow. Do not try to worm around them or come up with a justification as to why it's 'possible', for it is not.
  • The Teeth do not die.
  • The Teeth do not receive injuries or criminal charges that make them unfit for further service. They do not lose eligibility to acquire and maintain a security clearance. It may be deactivated due to a career change, but it is never revoked.
  • The Teeth do not change their identities.
  • The Teeth do not contact anyone involved in Cornucopia House or the defusing of the situation in 2016. At this point, they may contact other Teeth.
  • The Teeth do not recount the events of Cornucopia House and the affair to anyone who is not cleared to know. Whatever their exposure to such horror results in, it does not leave the confines of their mind and/or create new evidence against them.
  • The Teeth do not go bankrupt or achieve extreme financial success.
  • The Teeth, if went abroad, are back to a domestic posting by May 1, 2026 at the latest. They remain within the United States after this cutoff date. Likewise, the cutoff date for home pursuits is the same.
  • The Teeth do not come in contact with the genuine unnatural on their own accord. The operations in which they have encountered and survived the unnatural are exceptional, and can not be constructed by the players.
  • The Teeth can use stay on case action, but risk punishment if they are found to be doing it on other people without briefing the Program. However, at this point some of them may be desperate to learn the truth of what brought them to this, and chasing after that will have no penalties.
